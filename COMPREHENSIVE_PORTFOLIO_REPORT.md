## Appendix B: Calculation Methodologies

**Contamination Savings Calculation:**
- Current contamination charges identified from invoice line items
- Percentage of total spend calculated
- Industry benchmark: <3% of total spend
- Conservative savings estimate: 50% reduction achievable with education + monitoring
- Monthly savings: (Current annual contamination charges × 0.5) / 12

**Cost Per Door (CPD):**
- Formula: Total Monthly Cost / Number of Units
- Data source: Spend by Category sheet aggregated by property

**Yards Per Door (YPD):**
- **For Compactors:** (Total Tons × 14.49) / Units
- **For Dumpsters:** (Quantity × Size × Frequency × 4.33) / Units
- Data source: Service Details sheet
