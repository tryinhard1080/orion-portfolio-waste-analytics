## How to Use This Workbook

### For Portfolio Analysis
1. **Start with Executive Summary** - Get high-level KPIs
2. **Review Property Overview** - Understand portfolio composition
3. **Analyze Spend Summary** - Identify high/low CPD properties

### For Property-Specific Analysis
1. **Go to individual property tab** (8-17)
2. **Review Spend by Category** for that property
3. **Check Service Details** (if available)
4. **Compare Yards/Door** to benchmarks

### For Contract Management
1. **Review Contract Terms tab**
2. **Note expired/expiring contracts**
3. **Calculate notice deadlines**
4. **Plan contract negotiations**

### For Optimization
1. **Compare CPD across properties** in Spend Summary
2. **Review Yards/Door** - look for over-servicing
3. **Analyze Spend by Category** - identify fee reduction opportunities
4. **Check Extra Pickups and Overages** - right-size service
