## UNIQUE REGULATORY SITUATIONS IDENTIFIED

### üö® Phoenix: Recycling Prohibition
**Situation:** Phoenix is one of few major U.S. cities that PROHIBITS (rather than mandates) multifamily recycling.

**Ordinance:** Phoenix City Code Section 27-21(c)
- Restricts city from offering recycling to 30+ unit complexes
- State law (ARS ¬ß9-511.03, 2015) prevents cities from requiring multifamily recycling
- Recycling is VOLUNTARY ONLY

**Impact on Properties:**
- Bella Mirage (715 units): NO recycling compliance required
- Mandarina (180 units): NO recycling compliance required
- Properties CAN offer recycling as amenity (private contract)

**Strategic Consideration:**
- Evaluate resident demand for recycling amenity
